Welcome to Growth Tutorials

At Growth Tutorials, we are dedicated to unlocking the full potential of every student through comprehensive and personalized educational experiences. About Us

Our History

Growth Tutorials was founded in 2003 by Prof. Aparna Tewary, a visionary educator with over three decades of experience in teaching Biology and preparing students for competitive exams like NEET. From its inception, Growth Tutorials has been committed to providing quality education and fostering an environment where students can excel academically and personally.

Our Philosophy

At Growth Tutorials, we believe that education is the cornerstone of personal and societal growth. Our teaching philosophy is centered on understanding the individual needs of our students and adapting our methods to help them realize their highest potential. We strive to instill a love of learning, critical thinking, and a scientific temperament in all our students.

Our Commitment

We are dedicated to maintaining a high standard of education and continuously updating our teaching practices and curriculum to align with the latest educational standards and technology. Our small class sizes ensure that each student receives personalized attention, making learning more effective and enjoyable.

Our Facilities

Our state-of-the-art facilities include a specialized Biology lab, a library rich with academic resources, and comfortable, tech-equipped classrooms that promote an optimal learning environment. We also provide extensive support for NEET preparation, ensuring that our students are well-prepared for one of the most challenging competitive exams in India.

Our Community

Growth Tutorials is more than just an educational institution; it is a vibrant community of educators, students, and parents dedicated to mutual growth and support. We encourage our students to engage in community service and extracurricular activities, which helps them develop a balanced personality and a sense of responsibility towards their community.

Courses Offered

Science Education for 5th to 12th Grades

At Growth Tutorials, we offer comprehensive science education spanning grades 5 to 12. Our curriculum is designed to cater to students from various boards including IGSE, ICSE, CBSE, and State Boards. Each course is structured to deepen scientific knowledge and enhance practical understanding through rigorous theory classes and extensive laboratory experiments.

Specialized NEET Preparation

Our NEET preparation program is tailored to meet the needs of aspiring medical students. This rigorous course includes detailed study of Biology, Chemistry, and Physics, aligning with the latest NEET syllabus. We utilize a blend of traditional teaching methods and modern educational technology to provide students with a competitive edge. Regular mock tests, performance reviews, and personalized coaching are integral parts of our NEET preparation strategy.

Mathematics and Computing

For students passionate about mathematics and technology, we offer specialized courses designed to develop critical thinking and problem-solving skills. Our curriculum covers advanced mathematics and introduces students to basic and intermediate concepts in computer science, preparing them for various competitive exams and future academic pursuits in engineering and technology.

Languages and Social Sciences

Understanding the importance of holistic education, we also provide classes in English, Marathi, and other regional languages, along with courses in History, Geography, and Civics. These courses are designed to improve linguistic skills and help students gain a broader understanding of the world and its cultures.

Personalized Tutoring and Small Group Sessions

Recognizing that each student has unique learning needs, our courses are conducted in small groups with no more than 5 to 6 students each session. This allows for personalized tutoring and ensures that each student receives the attention they need to excel. Our experienced educators are committed to adapting their instructional strategies to meet the learning styles and preferences of each student.
